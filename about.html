About Us

A family rooted in faith, growing in love, and reaching the world for Christ.

Our story

From a living room to a house of transformation

Word Transformation Church was founded in 2009 by Pastor James Boateng with just 12 people gathered in a living room in Accra, Ghana. With a burning conviction that God's Word has the power to change every life, he began preaching week after week — and the people kept coming.

Over the years, what started as a small gathering grew into a thriving community of over 500 members. We have seen families restored, lives transformed, and the Gospel carried into our city and beyond.

Today we are a multicultural, multigenerational church that believes the best is still ahead.

What We Believe

Our faith is grounded in the timeless truth of God's Word.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for all faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works, but by His finished work on the cross.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers for life and service.

The Resurrection

We believe in the bodily resurrection of Jesus Christ and the promise of eternal life for all who believe.

The Great Commission

We believe every believer is called to make disciples of all nations and to be a witness for Christ.

Our Leadership

Shepherds called to serve and equip the body of Christ.

Pastor James Boateng

Senior Pastor & Founder

Pastor James has been in ministry for over 20 years. He holds a degree in Theology from the University of Ghana and is passionate about expository preaching and discipleship.

Pastor Grace Boateng

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry

Grace is a gifted teacher and counsellor with a heart for women and families. She leads the Women's Ministry and oversees pastoral care across the church.

Elder Sarah Mensah

Associate Pastor

Elder Sarah oversees the small groups ministry and mid-week Bible studies. She brings a deep love for Scripture and a gift for creating community.

Rev. Kwame Asante

Youth & Outreach Pastor

Rev. Kwame leads the Youth Ministry and community outreach programmes. His dynamic style and genuine love for young people has grown the youth department significantly.
