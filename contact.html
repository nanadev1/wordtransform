Getting Here

By Car
Take the Accra–Tema Motorway and exit at the East Legon interchange. Head north on the main East Legon road, then turn right onto Church Avenue. Parking is available on-site.
By Bus / Trotro
Take any trotro heading to East Legon from Accra Central or Madina. Alight at the East Legon main junction. The church is a 5-minute walk down Church Avenue.
Uber / Bolt
Search "Word Transformation Church, East Legon, Accra" on Uber or Bolt. Both services are widely available in the area.
Parking
Free parking is available in our compound and on the street. Parking attendants are on duty during Sunday services. Arrive 15 minutes early for best availability.
