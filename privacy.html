Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: March 2025

Word Transformation Church ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our ministry services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when voluntarily submitted through forms (e.g., contact, membership, prayer requests).
  • Giving & Donation Data: Payment information processed through our secure giving portal. We do not store full payment credentials on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected via analytics tools to improve user experience.
  • Prayer Requests: Any personal details you share when submitting a prayer request. These are treated with the utmost confidentiality.
  • Volunteer & Ministry Applications: Information provided when applying to serve in a ministry or small group.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process membership registrations and giving donations.
  • Send church newsletters, event announcements, and service updates (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • Respond to prayer requests, inquiries, and contact form submissions.
  • Improve our website functionality and content.
  • Facilitate volunteer coordination and small group placements.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers (e.g., payment processors, email platforms) who assist us in operating our website and ministry, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our congregation or the public.
  • Church Leadership: Pastoral staff may access prayer requests and sensitive matters strictly on a need-to-know basis for ministry purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our giving portal uses SSL encryption for all financial transactions. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how visitors use our site. You may configure your browser to refuse cookies; however, some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media platforms, streaming services). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal obligations.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
