Frequently asked questions
Do I need to be baptised to join?
You do not need to be baptised to apply. However, we encourage all members to take the step of baptism as a declaration of faith. We will journey with you through this.
Is membership free?
Yes — membership at Word Transformation Church is completely free. We believe giving should come from the heart, not as a condition of belonging.
How long does the process take?
After submitting your application, our team will contact you within 3–5 days. You will then be invited to a New Members' Class (usually 4 sessions) before formal membership is confirmed.
Can I transfer from another church?
Absolutely. We honour your spiritual history and journey. Please mention your previous church in the application and we will guide the transition respectfully.
Can I bring my whole family?
Yes! Select the Family Membership option and your spouse and children will be included. Children under 12 are automatically enrolled in our Children's Ministry.

Have questions before you apply? Our team is happy to chat.
