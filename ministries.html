Our Ministries

Every person has a place to belong, grow, and serve in God's family.

Ages 13–35

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where young people encounter God, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose. We believe the youth are not the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today.

  • Friday Youth Rally — every Friday, 5:00 PM
  • Annual Youth Camp — August
  • Discipleship classes and mentorship
  • Drama, music, and arts teams
Join Youth Ministry Contact Leader

Women's Ministry

Women of Transformation

A community where women of all ages are empowered, equipped, and encouraged in their faith, families, and calling. We celebrate womanhood through the lens of Scripture and create safe spaces for authentic connection.

  • Monthly Prayer Breakfast
  • Annual Women's Conference
  • Bible study and book clubs
  • Mentorship and counselling support
Join Women's Ministry Contact Leader

Men's Ministry

Men of Valour

Men of Valour exists to raise godly men who lead well in their homes, workplaces, and communities. We create a brotherhood where men can be transparent, accountable, and challenged to grow in every area of life.

  • Annual Men's Conference
  • Monthly brotherhood meetups
  • Leadership and fatherhood seminars
  • Community service projects
Join Men's Ministry Contact Leader

Ages 0–12

Children's Ministry

We believe the foundation of faith is built early. Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, joyful, and biblically grounded environment where children learn about God's love through stories, songs, crafts, and play.

  • Sunday Kids Church — parallel to main service
  • Vacation Bible School — every July
  • Children's choir and drama
  • Trained and vetted volunteer teachers

Worship Ministry

Worship & Creative Arts

Our Worship Ministry leads the congregation into the presence of God each Sunday and at every church gathering. We are a team of musicians, vocalists, sound engineers, and visual artists who serve with excellence and humility.

  • Sunday worship team
  • Choir — rehearsals every Thursday
  • Media, sound, and AV team
  • Drama and creative arts team

Missions & Outreach

Community Outreach

We are called to be the hands and feet of Jesus beyond our church walls. Our outreach team actively serves the city of Accra and supports mission work across Ghana and West Africa.

  • Monthly food distribution drives
  • Street evangelism teams
  • Prison and hospital visitation
  • Support for missionaries in the field
