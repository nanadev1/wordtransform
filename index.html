Who We Are

A Family That Believes
in Every Person

We started as a small gathering of people who believed the Bible still has the power to change lives — and that conviction has never left us. Over 15 years, God has grown us into a multigenerational family of 500+ members from across Accra and beyond.

We're not a perfect church. We're a church full of people in progress, walking together, growing together, and believing God for the impossible together.

"The Word of God is not a religious text — it is a living encounter. Every Sunday, every Wednesday, every prayer night, we come because we expect to meet God, and He has never let us down."

— Pastor Emmanuel, Senior Pastor
