About Us

About our church family

Our Mission

To transform lives through the power of God's Word and create a community of believers who impact their world for Christ.

We believe in the transformative power of Scripture and its ability to change hearts, heal wounds, and restore hope. At Word Transformation Church, we're committed to creating an environment where everyone can encounter God and experience genuine life change.
